Brownvase: Securing Investment through Strategic Branding and Web Design

Project Overview

  MIUX Studio partnered with Brownvase to develop a fintech platform designed for healthcare professionals. The project aimed to create an intuitive dashboard and refresh the brand identity, enhancing user experience, reducing cognitive load, and boosting investor appeal.

Key Insight

Efficiency and Clarity

Healthcare professionals value efficiency, clarity, and immediate financial insights.

Trust and Simplicity

Trust and simplicity are crucial in healthcare-focused fintech.

Visual Storytelling & Brand Cohesion

A cohesive brand identity significantly impacts user and investor engagement.

Strategic Visual Identity

We crafted a modern, purposeful logo—an abstract vase symbolizing stability and community—paired with an earthy palette reflecting wellness, warmth, and growth.

Design Challenge

Brownvase faced UX issues, including an unclear dashboard and a weak brand identity. MiUX Studio redesigned the fintech dashboard and branding, aligning closely with user needs and business goals.

Notable Achievements

Strategic Dashboard UX

Demonstrated the impact of user-centered design in specialized fintech solutions, driving user engagement and tangible business outcomes.

Brand Transformation

Created a modern, flexible logo and grounded palette symbolizing stability and growth.

Accelerator Acceptance

Secured acceptance into Gusto and Peachscore accelerator programs post-redesign.
